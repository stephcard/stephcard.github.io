Stephany Cardet

Human-first design engineer.

Stephany Cardet is a human-first design engineer. Working at the intersection content, design, and technology, Stephany is passionate about building trust, providing utility, and sharing stories with a focus on inclusivity and accessibility.

Stephany currently works at International Business Machines. She holds an MFA in Web Design and New Media from the Academy of Art University.

Design, HTML, CSS, and Javascript are her languages. Simplicity wins is her mantra.

State Magazine

The Webby Awards 2018 Honoree: Best Individual Editorial Experience.

State Magazine aims to capture this moment in America's political culture, which is exhilarating for those unnerved by what they see as the country's progressive, globalist tilt under Obama and anxiety-provoking for those who unexpectedly find that approach locked out of power in Washington.

Undocumented

After the factory where Miguel Gonzalez worked was raided by immigration agents, he feared he’d be deported and moved his family back to Mexico. Now Miguel struggles to get by and his daughter Casandra, who was born in the U.S., must learn to live in a country that she had never before called home.

Art direction by Jeffrey Hsu and Damian Prado.

Picture This

The Webby Awards 2016 Honoree: Best Use of Photography.

A photography-led experience featuring the last works of legendary photographer and photojournalist Mary Ellen Mark. CNN Money traveled with a legend to New Orleans to photograph key figures in the untold stories that will forever haunt NOLA: Hurricane Katrina. Our cameras are there as she captures these icons against the backdrop of a city that's still trying to heal, a decade later.

Art direction by Greg Chen.

Role: UI and UX; HTML, CSS, and Js.
