Stephany Cardet

Product, Ux design, and front-end code. Human-first, always.

Stephany is passionate about delivering strategic, scalable experiences and solutions that engage audiences, provide utility, and share stories.

Through human-first design practice, she navigates the intersection of product, user experience, and front-end web development to meet business KPIs.

Stephany is a Product & Ux Lead at IBM. She holds a master's degree in Web Design and New Media from the Academy of Art University.

She also is a distinguished faculty member at General Assembly.
