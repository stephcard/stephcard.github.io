Stephany Cardet

Human-first experience design.

Working at the intersection of content, design, and technology, Stephany is a seasoned Ux practitioner.

She is passionate about building trust, providing utility, and delivering high engagement experiences centered around users and key performance indicators (KPIs).

Stephany is a Ux Director at IBM. She holds a master's degree in Web Design and New Media from the Academy of Art University.

She also is a distinguished faculty member at General Assembly.
